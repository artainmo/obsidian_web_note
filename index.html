I need to buy via a scientific research company for legal reasons (1). I can create a Sole Proprietorship in Belgium easily to resolve this issue. Later you can convert to a Private Limited Company for protection from personal liability and to scale the company's personnel.

If I create this company, it could be of use for medical research (1), consultations (2), and the selling of chemical technology (3, 4).
When you incorporate, you need to declare your professional activity via a NACE code. The suggested NACE code combination for my activity is: '72.11 – Research in biotechnology' (medical/biomedical focus); '72.19 – Other natural sciences and engineering R&D' (for flexibility in chemistry/engineering); '74.90 – Other professional, scientific, and technical activities' (for consulting/advisory); '46.75 – Wholesale of chemical products' (for selling/licensing chemical technology).
You can describe your sole proprietorship in French like that: “Je souhaite enregistrer une entreprise individuelle ayant pour activité la recherche et le développement scientifique et médical (NACE 72.11 et 72.19), complétée par des services de consultation scientifique (NACE 74.90), et la commercialisation de technologies chimiques (NACE 46.75).”, or in Dutch: "Ik wil een eenmanszaak registreren met als activiteit wetenschappelijk en medisch onderzoek en ontwikkeling (NACE 72.11 en 72.19), aangevuld met wetenschappelijke consultancy (NACE 74.90) en de commercialisatie van chemische technologie (NACE 46.75).".
It is illegal to do medical consultations without a medical license, thus it is important to specify 'research consultations' and not say 'medical consultations' (5).

To incorporate I need to register with a business one-stop shop (ondernemingsloket / guichet d’entreprises). Bring: ID card; proof of professional address (can be your home); description of activities (with NACE codes). They will: register your sole proprietorship in the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (KBO/BCE); assign you an enterprise number. Also ask for a VAT number as this makes you appear more legitimate. Subsequently, create a bank account for the enterprise that is separate from your personal account, or for sole proprietorship you can reuse your personal bank account.

I used xerius to easily incorporate online on 15 September 2025.
I used the company name artainmo. I used an eenmanszaak which means the company can only contain one person whereon all the responsibilities fall. But I can change the company structure later when necessary.
As an enterprise who makes less than €25000 a year, I asked to not pay VTA. However, if in the end I do make more than that, I will need to contact my 'btw-controlekantoor' immediately.
To pay less social security, I indicated an expected income of less than €4391 for this year. If you make more than that you will need to pay a penalty. To limit the penalty, you need to notify about your higher income as fast as possible. Else, you need to indicate your expected income again each year, so you can adapt this on a yearly basis either way.
To sell chemical technologies you need certain authorizations, which is more complicated. This is why the company will for now not have that as an activity. I can add this activity later when I will actually be selling products.
In a couple of days, Xerius will have created the sole proprietorship. I can follow this process here.
