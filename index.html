Project

Write a scientific article in neuroscience. Contemplate if prefrontal dysfunction is a contributing factor in psychosis or schizophrenia.
The goal is for the theory to hold true after literature review and the article to be published in a serious scientific journal.

Used technologies

Google, Pubmed, and Unpaywall for literature search.
Obsidian for taking notes allowing idea development and specific literature reviews.
Microsoft Word for writing the article.
Grammarly for spell checking.
Discord for team communication.

Steps and features

  • Idea development:
    • Prefrontal deactivation contributes to psychosis.
    • Open potassium and HCN channels contribute to prefrontal deactivation.
    • Open potassium and HCN channels contribute to psychosis and schizophrenia.
