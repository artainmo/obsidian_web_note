Methylene Blue

How to use

For attentional and cognitive/memory enhancement methylene blue should be used at 5-25mg.

It has a half-life of 5-6hours while peak plasma levels occur 1-2hours after intake. I think this means it can be taken three times a day, with initial dose being a complete dose and next two doses being half of initial dose.

If I weight 60kg I would need an initial dose of 30mg-240mg. And subsequent doses of 15mg-120mg. Maybe 100mg capsules would be a good aim whereby I take two of them as first dose and one for two next doses. If I only take 30mg I am afraid the memory-enhancement to be absent even if some sources claim 20mg a day to be sufficient.
